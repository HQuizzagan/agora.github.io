Research Proposal Writing

Have an idea for research but unsure how to craft a compelling proposal? Going crazy trying to pin down the best title for your research? Too many ideas cluttering your brain? AGORA provides the “Research Proposal Writing” course for students across all levels who may need a guide in writing a coherent research framework. AGORA’s mentors will let you in on the tips and tricks of generating, sorting, and designing research ideas, and finally, writing a definitive title. Designed as an enriching exchange between mentor and mentee, the course will help you articulate your ideas, convince a panel, and sharpen your critical thinking. Avail this program in either crash course or consultation format and give yourself a head start this coming academic year. Inquire now to get started!
